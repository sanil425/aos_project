The Breath Behind the Game

How athletes breathe through motion

About the project: Inspired by Edgerton’s high speed imagery, we look at breath as the quiet engine of sport: the held inhale at impact, then the exhale that lets motion flow. The final piece will be a short, slow motion film with recorded breathing and brief athlete reflections. Below are some piece previews, we will be able to click any tile to view details.

Preview: This is our friend playing tennis, you can almost feel that split second before contact, the little breath he holds and lets go as the ball leaves the strings. We’ll swap this photo for a slow motion clip soon. We’ll record his real breath and short reflection on how he finds rhythm.
